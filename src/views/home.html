Why should I check?

Safety

All ingredients proved to be dangerous for your health are highlighted by red color. Yellow highlights are used for ingredients that may cause the allergic reaction. Green highlights mean that an ingredient is totally safe.

Effectiveness

Effecteveness is crucial! Purchasing any skincare product, we expect it to improve our skin condition (oily skin, wrinkles, redness etc.). The effective product should contain specific ingredients that target your problems effectively.

How to use SmartIngredients?


Beginner


Every product has smart ingredients list. Useful ingredients are marked with a symbol. If a product has at least 4 of them, it will be useful for your skin.

Even more simple? - There are charts reflecting safety, effectiveness and naturallity of a product after each ingredients list.

Pro


Put the mouse over any ingredient in a smart list and a pop-up message containing more info will appear.

Even more simple? - You will see the number of ingredients by functions after each ingredients list (moisturizers, mattifyers, etc.). Click on them and they will be highlighted in yellow.
